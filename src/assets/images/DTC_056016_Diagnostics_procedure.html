Confidentiality level : Internal
DTC 056016 - Voltage too low Diagnostics procedure
Step 1 Check Fuse
* Turn the ignition OFF.
* Visually check the condition of the fuse (Fuse should not be blown)
* Check the continuity of the fuse terminals by using multimeter.
Is the Fuse, okay?
YES ==> Go to step 2
NO ==> Replace the fuse. Click here
Step 2 Check the power supply circuit
* Disconnect the Rain sensor harness connector.
* Turn the ignition ON.
* Check the power supply at rain sensor harness connector terminals.
Connector: T42 Click here
Terminals: Between 32 and 34 Click here
Is the voltage value between 5V -7V?
YES ==> Go to step 4
NO ==> Go to Step 3
Step 3 Check the conformity of the rain sensor wiring harness
Check the continuity, insulation check (12V or earth) for the wiring
harness.
Connector: T42 Click here
Terminals: Between 32 and 34 Click here
ECU: BCM
Is the test result, okay?
YES ==> Go to step 4
NO ==> Repair the wiring harness connector.
Step 4 Check the rain sensor
Check the resistance of the rain sensor.
Is the resistance value between 25 ohms - 45 Ohms?
YES ==> Replace the BCM. Click here
NO ==> Replace the Rain sensor. Click here
